Our Story

A Culinary Journey Through India

Founded in 2010, Spice Symphony brings together the rich culinary traditions of North India with innovative fusion touches. Our chef, Rajiv Kapoor, trained in the royal kitchens of Rajasthan and the bustling streets of Delhi, creates dishes that tell a story.

We source our ingredients locally and sustainably, ensuring each dish not only tastes authentic but also supports our community. Our spices are freshly ground daily, and our tandoor fires burn with aromatic woods to impart that distinctive smoky flavor.

At Spice Symphony, we believe dining is an experience - from the warm ambiance to the carefully curated wine pairings, every detail is designed to transport you to the vibrant heart of India.
